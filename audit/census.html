The radius fault line — 2px vs 3px

The single most pervasive drift. .btn renders at 3px; .icon-act, .seg, .mono-btn, .btn-ink in the same shared file render at 2px (asterlogos.css:108,117,126,143), and pages hardcode 2px for chips/menus/nav. On one screen, corners disagree.

--control-r · 3px (canonical)
2px (12 sites in the reading prototype)
Side by side the difference is small but real — 2px reads a hair sharper/cheaper against a paper aesthetic built on soft letterpress. Route every radius through var(--control-r).
